zonnebrand — Solar panel export-limit controller for SMA inverters

zonnebrand is a Python tool that automatically protects your SMA solar inverter during negative electricity price windows. It monitors real-time EPEX spot prices and adjusts the two export-limit parameters in ennexOS to 0 % when prices go negative and restores them to 100 % when prices recover — fully automated, every 15 minutes.

Support

Yes! This library is entirely free but it runs on coffee. Your ❤️ is important to keep maintaining this package. You can support in various ways — have a look at the sponsor page.

Quick install

pip install zonnebrand

python zonnebrand.py --provider zonneplan --mail your_mail@gmail.com
